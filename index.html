Control-chart analysis for football possession dynamics.

Possession Signal SPC applies process-control thinking to minute-level football data, detecting unusual possession behavior and interpreting shifts around goals, cards, and penalties.

What the analysis covers

The project turns live match observations into interpretable control-chart signals, then connects those signals back to match events and outcomes.

Fixture scoring

Matches are ranked by events and out-of-control behavior to select high-signal fixtures.

Control limits

Home and away possession rates are compared against center lines and control bounds.

Event interpretation

Goals, cards, and penalties are reviewed as possible causes of delayed possession shifts.
